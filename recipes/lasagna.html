LASAGNA

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

It is baked until golden and bubbling, making it a comforting and delicious family meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 40 minutes.
  5. Let cool before serving.
