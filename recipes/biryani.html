BIRYANI

Description

Biryani is a flavorful South Asian rice dish made with basmati rice, spices, and meat or vegetables.

It is layered and cooked slowly to blend the aromatic flavors.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or mutton
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Garam masala
  • Saffron

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat with spices and yogurt.
  2. Cook the rice until partially done.
  3. Layer rice and meat in a pot.
  4. Cook on low heat for 20–25 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.
