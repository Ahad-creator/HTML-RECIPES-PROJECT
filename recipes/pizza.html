PIZZA

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flatbread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

It is baked in a hot oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese melts.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. Roll out the dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
